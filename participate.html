How To Participate

We are looking for participants aged 15-35 from the Newcastle area who:

  • are free from chronic neurological or psychiatric conditions,
  • have no metal implants, do not suffer from claustrophobia and are not pregnant,
  • are available for three testing sessions
    • two at the University of Newcastle
    • one at the Calvary mater hospital

For information about participating, please read the relevant information sheet:

  • Information sheet for participants under 18 years of age
  • Information sheet for participants aged 18 years or older

For examples of some of the questions we will ask you, please see these exemplars:

  • WHO Quality of Life Questionnaire
  • Sensation Seeking Scale Questionnaire

All participants will need to complete the relevant consent form before participating:

  • Consent Form for participants under 18 years of age
  • Consent Form for participants aged 18 years or older

If you would be interested in participating please contact us.
