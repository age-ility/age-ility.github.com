Sounds Interesting?

We are currently recruiting participants aged 15-35 years from the Newcastle area.

Participants need to be available to attend three testing sessions:

  • two at the University of Newcastle
  • one at the Calvary Mater Hospital

Participants are reimbursed $20/hr for time commitment and travel costs (max 10 hours). Parking is available at both sites.

To be eligible to participate, you must:

  • not have a chronic neurological or psychiatric condition,
  • not have any permanent metal implants,
  • not suffer from claustrophobia and
  • not be pregnant.
